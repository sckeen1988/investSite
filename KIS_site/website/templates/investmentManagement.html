The General Approach

Most big box investment opportunities available to the general public are not focused on how to safely get you the best returns possible, but instead on selling you financial services that make sense for you at your current stage in life. KIS was created to give more people access to a better system.

The Stock Market Reigns Supreme

Historically the stock market has significantly better returns than any other investment. Over a long time frame (10 years) the consistency of performance is actually quite good too. In particular, US large cap stocks have demonstrated consistent returns with limited downside. This is why the bedrock of my strategies are a modified world index, heavily weighted toward US large cap stocks.

Hedging with Derivatives

Simply investing in a few ETFs that encapsulate this index will probably greatly improve your portfolio's performance. The drawback to this approach is the uncertainty of returns and risk of the market. To mitigate this, KIS constantly hedges all positions with various options strategies geared at generating consistent income. This is a well proven approach that increases returns in all cases other than when the market is averaging returns in excess of 30% annually.

Annual Passive Income

In addition to annual growth commensurate with market performance, KIS targets an annual cash distribution of 5-7% to each investor. This distribution can be reinvested or used as income. This distribution makes KIS perfect for those in retirement looking for income while growing wealth, and those responsibly working towards building for retirement, and those interested in giving a large charitable donation annually. Important to note here that the cash distributions have potential tax implications.
